# Fishing Maxxed Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-29

Fishing Maxxed is built by Maxxed Technical Systems as an offline-first catch journal. This policy explains what information the app handles and how that information is used.

## Information the app handles

Fishing Maxxed may store the following information on your device when you create catch records:

- Catch photos you take in the app
- Species selections and manual species confirmations
- Estimated or manually entered fish length
- Optional weight, method/gear, notes, and catch status
- Optional location information if you grant location permission
- Broad public region used for privacy-safe records and exports

## How information is used

The app uses catch information to maintain your private journal, estimate fish length from a known-size reference, show uncertainty/confidence labels, support local personal rankings, and create user-initiated exports.

## Local storage

Fishing Maxxed is designed to store catch records locally on your device. The baseline app does not require an account, does not provide cloud sync, and does not publish global leaderboards.

## Photos

Photos are used for your private catch record and optional length estimation. You can also log catches manually without a photo.

## Location

Location is optional. If you grant location permission, exact coordinates may be stored in your private local catch record. Default exports are designed to exclude exact coordinates and use a broad public region instead.

## Sharing and exports

Fishing Maxxed does not share your catch records automatically. Exports and Android share actions are initiated by you. Review exported files before sending them to someone else.

## Regulation guidance

Fishing Maxxed does not provide authoritative fishing regulation decisions. The bundled regulation fixture is limited and directs users to official agency regulations. Always check official regulations before keeping fish.

## Deleting information

You can delete individual catch records in the app. You can also remove app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage through Android settings.

## Third-party services

The current baseline does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, public account systems, or cloud sync. If those features are added later, this policy and the Play Console data-safety declaration must be updated before release.
