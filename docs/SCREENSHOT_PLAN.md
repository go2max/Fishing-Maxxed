# Fishing Maxxed Screenshot Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-29

## Device setup

- Use a clean install or clear app data before capture.
- Prefer a modern 1080p+ Android device.
- Use dark system mode to match the app's default visual tone.
- Turn on airplane mode if possible to reinforce offline-first behavior.
- Do not expose real exact fishing coordinates in screenshots.

## Seed data

Create three sample records for screenshots:

1. Largemouth bass, 14.25 in, local catch, Sacramento Valley, note: `Morning shoreline catch.`
2. Rainbow trout, 11.50 in, released, Sierra foothills, note: `Released after quick photo.`
3. Unknown species, no measurement, unverified, Region withheld, note: `Manual note only.`

Use broad public regions only.

## Required screenshots

### 1. Capture start

Show the Capture tab with either the camera permission panel or a live camera preview. The value is proving the app has a clear first-run action.

Callout for store caption: `Capture a catch photo or log manually.`

### 2. Measurement editor

Show a captured image with fish/reference handles visible and the measurement result text displayed.

Callout: `Estimate length with a known-size reference.`

### 3. Species confirmation

Show species search results and the confirmed-by-user message.

Callout: `Confirm species before saving.`

### 4. Journal summary

Show saved records, summary stat cards, filter field, and export button.

Callout: `Private catch journal on your device.`

### 5. Expanded record

Show an expanded record with notes, status chips, rule summary, and `Exact coordinates: private` copy.

Callout: `Edit details without exposing private coordinates.`

### 6. Regulation warning

Show the Rules screen with `Unable to verify - check official regulations` visible.

Callout: `Clear non-authoritative regulation warnings.`

### 7. Local leaderboard

Show measured catches ranked locally.

Callout: `Device-only leaderboard. No global ranking.`

### 8. Alternate theme

Toggle theme and show either Capture or Journal in the alternate palette.

Callout: `Choose the look that fits your style.`

## Optional promo graphic

Layout: phone mockup plus catch journal cards.

Headline: `Your private fishing log.`

Subhead: `Photo-assisted length estimates, redacted exports, and local-only rankings.`

## Screenshot guardrails

- Do not show real coordinates.
- Do not imply legal keeper authorization.
- Do not imply AI species recognition.
- Do not imply public/global competition.
- Do not show unsafe fish handling.
- Do not show protected species claims.
