## Full description

Fishing Maxxed is an offline-first Android catch journal for anglers who want a private, practical way to record catches on the water.

Log catches with a photo or manual entry, estimate fish length with a known-size reference, adjust measurement handles, confirm species manually, add notes, and keep a device-local leaderboard of your measured catches.

Built for privacy by default:

- Catch records stay on the device unless you export them.
- Exact coordinates are stored privately when location is enabled.
- Default CSV export uses broad public region and does not include exact coordinates.
- Leaderboards are local to your device, not public or global.

Fishing Maxxed also includes a limited regulation-checking demonstration screen. The bundled regulation fixture is not authoritative and cannot approve a fish as legal to keep. Always check official agency regulations before keeping, releasing, or transporting fish.

Core features:

- CameraX catch photo capture
- Known-reference length estimate
- Draggable fish and reference endpoints
- Manual no-photo catch logging
- Species confirmation
- Notes, method, status, weight, and broad region
- Local private journal
- Redacted CSV export
- Device-only leaderboard
- Non-authoritative regulation warning flow

Fishing Maxxed is designed for personal catch tracking, not legal advice, official regulation verification, or public competition.
