## Full description

Fishing Maxxed is an offline-first catch journal for anglers who want a simple private record of catches, measurements, notes, and local personal bests.

Capture a fish photo, use a known-size reference for an estimated length, adjust measurement handles, confirm the species manually, and save the catch to your on-device journal. You can also create a manual no-photo entry when a photo is not practical.

Fishing Maxxed is designed for personal tracking, not official compliance verification. Regulation output is intentionally limited and non-authoritative. Always check official fishing regulations before keeping a fish.

Core features:

- Photo capture with CameraX
- Known-reference length estimate
- Manual no-photo catch entries
- Draggable fish/reference correction handles
- Measurement uncertainty and confidence labels
- Manual species confirmation
- Private local catch journal
- Edit notes and delete records
- Default CSV export that uses broad public region instead of exact coordinates
- Device-local leaderboard for personal bests
- Clear non-authoritative regulation guidance

Privacy posture:

- Catch records are stored locally on the device.
- Exact coordinates are private by default.
- Default exports exclude exact coordinates.
- No account, cloud leaderboard, or public ranking is claimed.
