# Data Safety Draft

- Catch photos, notes, measurements, and optional exact coordinates are stored on device in app-private storage.
- The app contains no account, analytics, advertising, cloud sync, or global leaderboard backend.
- No personal data is transmitted or shared by the app automatically.
- User-initiated default CSV export excludes exact coordinates and private photo paths.
- Users delete individual records in the journal; uninstalling removes app-private data.
