# Fishing Maxxed Privacy Policy

Fishing Maxxed processes catch photos, measurements, notes, species confirmations, and optional location on your device. It does not upload this information to a Maxxed service and includes no analytics or advertising SDK. Exact coordinates are app-private and excluded from default exports. Sharing is initiated by the user through Android's share sheet.

Uninstalling removes app-private records and photos. Add the developer's verified support email and mailing address before Play submission.
