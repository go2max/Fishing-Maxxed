# Known Limitations

- The app is still a UI scaffold and does not implement the production fishing workflow.
- The displayed catch data, species suggestion, and region/rule status are placeholder UI content rather than results from a real measurement or regulation engine.
- No capture, species confirmation, journal management, or private-location workflow exists.
- No truthful redacted export or California regulation-package pipeline exists.
- No privacy enforcement exists for the required production location/rules workflow.
- Because the required product scope is missing, this release candidate is blocked for Play internal testing despite valid signed artifacts.
